Ocaml minor garbage collector: Syntax tree

I applied the ast_graph tool to the file byterun/minor_gc.c in the Ocaml 3.09.3 distribution. With about 15.000 nodes and 20.000 edges its syntax tree is not really bit. However, it still big enough to get most viewer software into trouble. On the right hand side you see a small part of it (about 5%) reduced to a 2000x1300 pixels. The line at the bottom of the page is the full tree, reduced to 8000x280.

You can download the following versions:

[minor_gc-detail.png.gz] detail (as on the right hand side) as 30150 x 19538 png
[minor_gc.ps.gz] complete tree, postscript
[minor_gc-pages.ps.gz] complete tree, postscript, paginated
[minor_gc.fig.gz] complete tree, xfig
[minor_gc.dot.gz] complete tree, dot

Please let me know, if you can display the whole graph in a better way than with gv or xfig (especially with more convenient scrolling and sooming).
